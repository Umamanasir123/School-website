FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What type of schoarships are available?

There are 2 types of Scholarship,Educational Assistance on Need and merit based scholarships.


Where can I go to discuss regarding the scholarships procedure?

You can email/call OR visit the Scholarships & Financial Aid section in the Admissions department to discuss.


What are my chances of gaining a scholarship?

If you apply for Educational Assistance on Need, you have to submit all the required documents mentioned in the form and a survey of your house will be conducted to judge your need and also you have to achieve those grades mentioned above.The merit scholarship is awarded if you are meeting the percetage criteria only and house survey is not required.


What should I submit with my Scholarship/Education Assistance application?
  • One passport size photograph
  • Current Income Proof of Parent/Guardian
  • Copy of last paid utility bills (Electricity, Telephone, Gas & Water)
  • Copy of rent agreement or receipt of rent payment if residing in a rented house
  • Copy of last result grade
  • Copy of CNIC of Parent/Guardian

If you are applying for a merit scholarship, the following documents are required:

  • One passport size photograph
  • Current registration slip
  • Overall result card

How long does it take to receive a scholarship?

It will take three to four months after submitting the scholarship application. Applications of Educational Assistance on Need and merit scholarship are presented to the Finance & Planning Committee (F&PC) meeting which meets twice in a year in the m/o May and October.
